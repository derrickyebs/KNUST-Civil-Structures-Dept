About the Structural Section

Excellence in structural engineering education and research since 1952

The Structural Engineering Section of the Department of Civil Engineering at KNUST has been at the forefront of structural engineering education and research in Ghana and West Africa for decades. Our faculty members are internationally recognized experts who contribute to advancing knowledge in structural analysis, design, and materials science.

We are committed to training the next generation of structural engineers who will design and build safe, sustainable, and innovative infrastructure for Africa and beyond. Our graduates work in leading construction firms, consulting engineering companies, research institutions, and government agencies across the globe.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education in structural engineering, conduct cutting-edge research that addresses local and global challenges, and produce graduates who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values to contribute meaningfully to society through the design and construction of safe, sustainable, and innovative infrastructure.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in structural engineering education and research in Africa, recognized internationally for our innovative contributions to sustainable infrastructure development and our commitment to addressing the unique challenges facing developing nations.

Research Facilities

Structures Laboratory

Our well-equipped structures laboratory features a universal testing machine, hydraulic actuators for cyclic loading, data acquisition systems, and strain measurement equipment. The facility supports research on structural components and materials testing.

Concrete Lab

The concrete laboratory is equipped for the preparation, curing, and testing of concrete specimens to assess both fresh and hardened properties. Facilities include batching and mixing equipment, slump and flow test apparatus, cube and cylinder moulds, curing tanks, compression testing machines, and durability testing setups for strength and quality evaluation.

Computational Lab

Our computational laboratory provides high-performance computing resources and licensed software for structural analysis including ANSYS, SAP2000, ETABS, and ABAQUS. Students and researchers have access to workstations for complex numerical simulations.
